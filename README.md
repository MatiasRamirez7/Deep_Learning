# Abstract

_Análisis de las características y elementos visuales de imágenes basado en Deep Learning para reconocer y predecir las emociones que transmite una imagen publicitaria._

<p align="justify">
"Overview

This thesis explores the use of Deep Learning models to analyze marketing images and evaluate their emotional and visual attractiveness. The study aims to connect visual marketing analysis with artificial intelligence in order to provide complementary tools for understanding customer preferences and needs.

Research Motivation

Advertising has evolved across cultures, historical periods, and social contexts, moving from simple text-based formats to sophisticated audiovisual productions. As consumer preferences and trends have changed, marketing strategies have also had to adapt in order to attract greater attention and engagement.

At the same time, technological advances have made it possible to segment consumers, study their behavior, understand purchasing decisions, and even predict them. In this context, this thesis seeks to contribute to the intersection between marketing and artificial intelligence.

Research Questions

This study is centered around the following questions:

Which marketing images generate greater emotional attraction?
What characteristics make these images visually appealing?
Methodology

To address these questions, this work explores Deep Learning, a subfield of Machine Learning. Deep Learning models are capable of learning patterns autonomously through a training process in which they are exposed to large numbers of examples and their corresponding correct outputs. Once trained with sufficient data, these models can generalize what they have learned and apply it to new data.

Objective

The main objective of this thesis is to evaluate whether deep neural network models can be used to identify emotionally attractive marketing images and to analyze the visual features that contribute to such attractiveness.
